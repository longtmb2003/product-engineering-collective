# Roadmap

> This document defines the long-term evolution of the Product Engineering Collective website.
>
> The goal is not to build everything immediately.
> Instead, we prioritize delivering a premium MVP first, then continuously expanding the platform.

---

# Vision

The website should evolve from a simple landing page into a complete digital hub for the collective.

Future visitors should be able to:

- Understand who we are
- Understand how we work
- Explore products
- Read engineering articles
- Learn from case studies
- Contact us
- Potentially hire us

---
